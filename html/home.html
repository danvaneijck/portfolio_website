Employment History
Research Assistant at Victoria University of Wellington

Employed at the Victoria University of Wellington as a research assistant for professor Alan Brent. I produced a document that explained how crypto-currency tokens are mined and processed via consensus mechanisms such as "Proof-of-Work", "Proof-of-Stake" and "Proof-of-Authority". The document also describes what "Decentralised autonomous organisations" (DAOs) are in detail and how they are different from traditional centralised organisations. Finally, the research explores how DAOs can be used to integrate and improve supply chain systems by providing a transparent platform where physical assets can be tracked and traded between stakeholders in the supply chain with the use of tokenomics.

Date: From December 2019 to January 2020
